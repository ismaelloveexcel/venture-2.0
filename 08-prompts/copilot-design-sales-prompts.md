# Copilot Prompts — Design & Sales Review

---

## Design QA Review

```
Review this page copy/design against my design QA checklist:
[paste copy, wireframe description, or screenshot description]

Check:
1. Copy & Messaging — is the headline clear in 5 seconds?
2. Visual Hierarchy — is there one clear CTA?
3. Trust signals — social proof, guarantee, contact info present?
4. Mobile readiness — would this work on a phone?
5. Brand consistency — tone and style consistent?

Flag every failure. Prioritise top 3 fixes.
```

---

## Improve a Headline

```
Improve this headline for a landing page targeting [niche]:
CURRENT: [paste headline]

Write 5 alternatives that:
- Pass the 5-second clarity test
- Include a specific outcome or timeframe
- Speak to the prospect's biggest fear or desire
- Avoid buzzwords and generic phrases
```

---

## Write Sales Email Subject Lines

```
Write 10 cold email subject lines for this offer: [describe offer]
Target: [niche and role]
Rules: under 8 words, no clickbait, make them curious or speak to a pain.
Format: subject line — one reason it works.
```

---

## Improve Sales Copy

```
Improve this sales copy for [niche]:
[paste copy]

Make it:
- More specific (add numbers, timeframes, outcomes)
- Less generic (remove all phrases a competitor could also use)
- More credible (add proof, specifics, remove hype)
- Action-driving (clear next step)

Show before/after for each change.
```

---

## Write a Proposal

```
Write a client proposal for:
- Client: [name/company]
- Their problem: [what they told you on the call]
- My solution: [what you'll deliver]
- Timeline: [weeks/months]
- Investment: [price]
- Guarantee: [risk reversal]

Format: executive summary, the problem, our solution, deliverables, timeline, investment, guarantee, next steps.
Keep it under 2 pages. Professional but conversational tone.
```
