What AuditBound actually does

System layers, not feature marketing.

A. ELIGIBILITY GATES

Defines who can be contacted at run time.

Applies suppression rules and pre-send validation before execution.

B. APPROVAL LAYER

Captures who approved what and exactly when.

Maintains a timestamped immutable decision record per cohort.

C. AUDIT TRAIL ENGINE

Writes append-only logs tied to run IDs.

Enables cohort reconstruction and full send-history traceability.
