## Where drift happened (plain words)

We spent energy on **“how not to screw up”** (stops, logs, terminology, contracts).  
That is **not the same** as spending the same hours on **“how to win”** (ICP sharpness, proof in the first line, pilot offer, call script, competitor story).

**The tool does not replace those.** It **contains risk** while you compete on **clarity and fit**.
