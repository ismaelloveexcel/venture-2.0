# Niche Scorecard — 8-Criteria Framework

Score every idea on each criterion from 1–10 before committing time to it.
**Threshold to proceed: 56/80 or higher.**

---

## How to Score

Open Copilot Chat and paste:

```
Score this idea using my 8-criteria niche scorecard (each out of 10):
IDEA: [your idea]

1. Pain Intensity — How urgent and painful is the problem? (1=nice-to-have, 10=emergency)
2. Niche Specificity — How targeted is the audience? (1=everyone, 10=one laser-focused persona)
3. Willingness to Pay — Would they pay $1,000+/month? (1=no budget, 10=already spending heavily)
4. Reachability — Can I find and contact 500+ prospects this week? (1=impossible, 10=easy list)
5. Speed to First $ — How fast can I close a paid client? (1=6+ months, 10=within 2 weeks)
6. Competitive Gap — Is there room for a better/different player? (1=saturated, 10=wide open)
7. AI Leverage — Can AI make delivery 10x faster or cheaper? (1=manual only, 10=fully AI-powered)
8. Personal Fit — Do I have skills, network, or credibility in this space? (1=zero, 10=strong advantage)

Give a score, one-line reason, and a TOTAL out of 80. Then give a GO / NO-GO verdict.
```
