1. Company name and URL
2. What they offer (specifically)
3. Price (if public)
4. Their positioning / target customer
5. Obvious gap or weakness in their offer
6. What I could do better or differently

List the top 5 competitors and then give me:
- The one gap I can exploit
- A positioning statement that differentiates me from all of them
