# Outreach Scripts

Copy, personalise, and send. Track results in prospects.csv.

---

## CREDIBILITY LAUNCH SYSTEM — LOCKED VALIDATION SCRIPT

Use this only for founder-led B2B service firms with a decent website and weak LinkedIn presence. Do not A/B test before 50 sends.

**Subject:** quick one

Hi [First Name],

Quick one — [Company] looks solid, but your LinkedIn presence doesn't yet reflect the quality of the offer.

That can quietly hurt trust when prospects check you before replying or booking.

I help founder-led B2B service firms fix that in 72 hours: positioning, profile cleanup, and first content so the company looks credible and active.

Want me to send the 3 things I'd change for [Company]?

Best,  
Ismael Sudally  
Auditbound

### Positive Reply Response

Sure — quick look:

1. [one positioning gap]
2. [one credibility gap]
3. [one content/activity gap]

Happy to walk you through how I'd fix this in 72 hours — does a 15-min call work this week?

## COLD EMAIL — Template A (Problem-Led)

**Subject:** [First Name] — quick question about [specific pain]

Hi [First Name],

I help [niche] businesses [specific outcome] — usually within [timeframe].

Most [niche] owners I talk to are dealing with [pain point]. Sound familiar?

If that's on your radar, I'd love to share a quick idea that worked for [similar company/type].

Worth a 15-minute call this week?

[Your Name]
[Phone / Calendly link]

## LINKEDIN DM — Template A (Opener)

Hi [First Name] — I work with [niche] to [outcome]. Noticed you're at [Company] — curious if [pain point] is something you're actively working on? Not pitching, just want to understand if it's even relevant to you.

## LINKEDIN DM — Template B (Follow-Up, Day 5)

Hey [First Name] — following up on my last message. Completely understand if timing's off. Just wanted to leave you with [one useful insight / resource relevant to their business]. Happy to connect either way.
