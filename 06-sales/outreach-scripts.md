# Outreach Scripts

Copy, personalise, and send. Track results in prospects.csv.

---

## COLD EMAIL — Template A (Problem-Led)

**Subject:** [First Name] — quick question about [specific pain]

Hi [First Name],

I help [niche] businesses [specific outcome] — usually within [timeframe].

Most [niche] owners I talk to are dealing with [pain point]. Sound familiar?

If that's on your radar, I'd love to share a quick idea that worked for [similar company/type].

Worth a 15-minute call this week?

[Your Name]
[Phone / Calendly link]

---

## COLD EMAIL — Template B (Result-Led)

**Subject:** How [Similar Company] got [specific result]

Hi [First Name],

[Result story in 2 sentences — real or hypothetical pilot result.]

I help [niche] do the same through [brief description of service].

If you're open to it, I can walk you through exactly how it works in 20 minutes.

Here's my calendar: [link]

[Your Name]

---

## LINKEDIN DM — Template A (Opener)

Hi [First Name] — I work with [niche] to [outcome]. Noticed you're at [Company] — curious if [pain point] is something you're actively working on? Not pitching, just want to understand if it's even relevant to you.

---

## LINKEDIN DM — Template B (Follow-Up, Day 5)

Hey [First Name] — following up on my last message. Completely understand if timing's off. Just wanted to leave you with [one useful insight / resource relevant to their business]. Happy to connect either way.

---

## FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE (after no reply)

| Day | Channel | Action |
|-----|---------|--------|
| 0 | Email/DM | Send initial message |
| 3 | Email | Follow-up: "Did this land?" |
| 7 | LinkedIn | Comment on their post + DM |
| 14 | Email | Break-up email: "Should I close your file?" |
