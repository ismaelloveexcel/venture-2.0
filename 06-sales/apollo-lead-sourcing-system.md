# Apollo Lead Sourcing System

## 90-Day Wedge ICP

Target existing B2B service businesses with enough deal value to justify a $5,500 setup and $1,350/month retainer.

## Primary Segments

| Segment | Why It Fits | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| IT/MSP firms | High-ticket retainers, referral dependency, clear local/regional markets | Break/fix-only shops |
| B2B agencies | Understand lead gen value, faster buying cycles | Tiny freelancers and low-ticket social media shops |
| Recruiters | Pipeline pain is constant, outbound is normal | Candidate-only recruiters with no client acquisition pain |
| Specialist consultants | High margin, founder-led sales | General coaches with unclear offer |

## Apollo People Search Filters

Use one segment per campaign. Do not mix all verticals in the first list.

### Titles

- Founder
- Co-Founder
- Owner
- CEO
- Managing Director
- Director of Sales
- Head of Sales
- Head of Growth
- VP Sales
- Chief Revenue Officer

### Company Headcount

- 2-10
- 11-50

Exclude 51+ for the first 30 days unless the offer is clearly founder-led and fast-moving.

### Geographies

Priority order:

1. United Kingdom
2. United States
3. Canada
4. Australia

Run separate campaigns by geography so proof and objections are easier to interpret.

### Industries / Keywords

Start with one of these clusters:

- Information Technology & Services, Managed IT, Cybersecurity, Cloud Consulting
- Marketing & Advertising, Digital Marketing, Performance Marketing, Web Design
- Staffing & Recruiting, Executive Search, Recruitment Agency
- Management Consulting, Business Consulting, Operations Consulting

### Company Signals

Prioritize prospects with at least one:

- Hiring sales, SDR, growth, or marketing roles
- Active LinkedIn company page
- Founder posts about growth, sales, leads, or referrals
- Clear service pages and case studies
- Uses HubSpot, Pipedrive, Apollo, Clay, Instantly, or similar tools

## Exclusions

- Gmail/Yahoo/Outlook personal domains
- Restaurants, retail, ecommerce-only, consumer local services
- Healthcare claims, legal services, or financial promotions without compliance review
- Businesses with no website or unclear offer
- Average deal size likely below $2,000

## First List Build

Build 150-200 leads in this order:

1. 75 IT/MSP owners in the UK
2. 75 B2B agency founders in the UK
3. 50 recruiters or specialist consultants as backup

Each row must include:

- Company
- Domain
- First name
- Last name
- Title
- Email
- LinkedIn URL
- Industry
- Pain signal
- Source URL or note

## Qualification Rules

Ready:

- Business domain email
- Decision-maker title
- Clear B2B service offer
- Likely $2,000+ average deal size

Review:

- Strong company fit but email missing
- Title is adjacent but not final buyer
- Geography or service line unclear

Reject:

- No company website
- Personal email only
- Low-ticket or consumer business
- No obvious commercial pain

## Outreach Hooks

IT/MSP:

```text
Most MSPs I see still rely heavily on referrals even though their service is recurring and high-trust. We build a low-volume outbound system that starts relevant conversations without turning the founder into a full-time prospector.
```

B2B agency:

```text
Agencies often build growth systems for clients while their own pipeline is still referral-heavy. We install a client-owned outbound system so new conversations do not depend on word of mouth alone.
```

Recruiter:

```text
Recruiters live or die by pipeline, but most outreach systems are scattered across spreadsheets, LinkedIn, and inboxes. We install a monitored outbound workflow for cleaner client-side conversations.
```
