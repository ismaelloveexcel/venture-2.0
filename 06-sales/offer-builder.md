# Offer Builder

Your offer is the most important thing you build. Do this before any code or design.

---

## The Offer Formula

> "I help [specific customer] achieve [specific outcome] in [timeframe] using [method], guaranteed [risk reversal]."

**Your draft:**

I help B2B service companies increase cold outreach reply rates by tailoring messaging to real prospect pain, guaranteeing at least 5 qualified replies within 14 days or we extend at no cost.

## Offer Builder Prompt

```
Build me an irresistible productized service offer using this information:

- Customer: [who exactly]
- Their pain: [what's wrong today]
- Outcome they want: [what does success look like in 90 days]
- My delivery method: [what I do for them]
- My unfair advantage: [why me over anyone else]

Create:
1. Offer name (clear, outcome-focused)
2. One-line pitch
3. What's included (5-7 specific deliverables)
4. Timeline (week 1, month 1, month 3)
5. Price recommendation (setup fee + monthly retainer)
6. Guarantee (reduce their risk)
7. The one objection this offer must overcome
```

## Your Offer Details

### Offer Name:

Cold Outreach Reply Acceleration (14-Day Pilot)

### One-Line Pitch:

Generate 5-10 qualified replies from 50 personalized outreach messages in 14 days using real prospect pain signals.

### What's Included:
1. Prospect research and pain signal identification (50 profiles)
2. Personalized message generation tied to real prospect challenges
3. Daily monitoring and reporting on reply rates
4. Call scheduling coordination for positive replies
5. End-of-pilot analysis and next-step recommendation 

### Timeline:
- **Week 1:** Prospect research, message generation, send batch 1 (50 messages)
- **Week 2:** Monitor replies, qualify conversations, book calls
- **End of Pilot (Day 14):** Report results and decide on scaling 

### Price:
- Pilot flat fee: $300
- Positioning: "This covers setup and execution for the 14-day pilot."
- No additional fees unless scaling beyond pilot

### Guarantee:

If we don't generate at least 5 qualified replies by day 14, we'll extend the pilot at no additional cost until we do.

### Main Objection & Rebuttal:
- Objection: "How do I know this will actually work for my business?"
- Rebuttal: "That's exactly why we run a pilot. Low risk, high signal. If it doesn't produce replies, we extend at no cost. If it does, you have proof it works."

# Pilot Template (7–14 Day Validation Sprint)

Use this to lock your pilot structure. Fill in all fields—no ambiguity on calls.

## Pilot Scope (ONE of each)

### Target Channel:

Cold email outreach with personalized messaging

### Target Segment:

B2B service companies (agencies, coaches, SaaS, local services) currently sending generic cold outreach with low reply rates

### Single Measurable Outcome:

Generate 5–10 qualified replies from 50 outbound messages within 14 days
- Binary: either we hit 5+ replies or we extend at no cost
- Observable: tracked in email platform and call calendar
- Tied to business value: each reply represents a potential client conversation

---

## Pilot Duration

- **Fixed: 14 days**
- Outcome: 5+ qualified replies from 50 sends
- If outcome not met, extend at no cost (only until outcome is hit)
- Never change duration once pilot starts

---

## Pricing Model (Pick One)

- Positioning: "This covers setup and execution for the pilot."
- Collect payment before work starts (no exceptions)

### Model B: Reduced Fee + Success Component (Do not use yet)

## Post-Pilot Transition

If the pilot succeeds (outcome achieved), define the path to scale:

**Script:**
"If we hit 5+ replies, we scale this into a full ongoing outreach system with done-for-you message management and continuous reply optimization."

Do NOT discuss pricing or retainer yet. Keep momentum on winning the pilot first.

---

## Call Positioning Formula (Exact Script)

Use this on every pilot close:

"We run a short pilot focused on generating qualified replies from your target prospects.

It takes 14 days, and by the end you'll know if this actually works for your business.

If we don't hit at least 5 qualified replies, we'll extend at no additional cost.

If we do, you have proof it works and we talk about scaling it."

**Alternative call close (stronger):**
"Would you be open to testing this for your outreach?"

---

## Post-Call Decision Matrix

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Strong pain + clear fit | Offer pilot immediately |
| Medium pain + engaged | Offer pilot with tighter scope |
| Weak or vague pain | Do not offer pilot; disqualify cleanly |
| Curious but no urgency | Disqualify (time waster) |

---

## Pilot Execution Checklist (Day 1)

After pilot is accepted:

- [ ] Send welcome email with scope, timeline, and success metric
- [ ] Lock calendar for daily/check-ins (async is OK)
- [ ] Define exact measurement criteria (not subjective)
- [ ] Confirm payment cleared before work starts

---

## Feedback Loop (Day 7 / Day 14)

At midpoint (Day 7):
- Show early signal or early warning
- Adjust scope if needed (tighten only, don't expand)

At endpoint (Day 14):
- Present results against outcome metric
- Make next decision: scale, pivot scope, or close
