## Offer Builder Prompt

```
Build me an irresistible productized service offer using this information:

- Customer: [who exactly]
- Their pain: [what's wrong today]
- Outcome they want: [what does success look like in 90 days]
- My delivery method: [what I do for them]
- My unfair advantage: [why me over anyone else]

Create:
1. Offer name (clear, outcome-focused)
2. One-line pitch
3. What's included (5-7 specific deliverables)
4. Timeline (week 1, month 1, month 3)
5. Price recommendation (setup fee + monthly retainer)
6. Guarantee (reduce their risk)
7. The one objection this offer must overcome
```
