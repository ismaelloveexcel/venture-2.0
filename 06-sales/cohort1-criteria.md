# Cohort 1 — criteria (Auditbound)

Outbound agency founders, early stage, thin audit trail. Used to source and QA the first **25-prospect** list (`cohort1-prospects-template.csv`).

---

## ICP definition

**Who**

- **Founder or co-founder** of an agency whose core offer is **outbound** (cold email, outbound SDR-as-a-service, or similar) for clients.
- **Agency age:** **0–18 months** since founding (incorporation, first paid client, or public “open for business” — pick one rule for the list and apply it consistently).
- **Client load:** **2–6 active retainer or project clients** for whom they run or oversee outbound (not pure brand-only shops).
- **Geography / language:** English-speaking outbound to US, UK, CA, or AU unless you intentionally narrow further.

**Why they fit Auditbound**

- They sell **trust and process** to clients but often lack a **single reconstructable trail** from “who was cleared to contact” → “who approved send” → “what actually sent.”
- The positioning hook: *When a client asks you to prove what happened in a campaign — can you reconstruct it in 60 seconds?*

---

## Trigger moment (why now)

Prioritize prospects where **at least one** is visible:

- Recent LinkedIn or site copy about **scaling outbound**, **new clients onboarded**, or **hiring** (capacity strain).
- Public case study, testimonial, or post admitting **volume** or **speed** without mentioning **controls** or **audit**.
- Founder post about **client questions**, **transparency**, or **firefighting** after a send.
- **No** requirement for a public “incident” — the trigger is **role + stage + service type**, with optional reinforcement from content.

---

## Exclusion rules (hard filters)

| Rule | Rationale |
|------|-----------|
| **Agency older than 3 years** | Out of cohort; Cohort 1 is deliberately **young** shops. |
| **Solo freelancer with 0 clients** | Exclude — no operating rhythm to reconstruct; not the same pain as a small agency bench. |
| **“Enterprise” positioning** | Exclude if the shop targets only Fortune 500 / global enterprise programs **and** shows dedicated compliance, security, or procurement pages typical of enterprise vendors (you are not selling an enterprise procurement story in v1). |
| **Not outbound-led** | Exclude pure paid media, SEO-only, or creative-only shops with no listed outbound / cold email offer. |
| **No clear founder-facing contact** | Skip if you cannot attribute a named founder + work email path (do not guess personal emails from patterns alone). |

**Solo with 1+ outbound clients:** borderline — default **exclude** for Cohort 1 to keep the list homogeneous; revisit in Cohort 2 if needed.
