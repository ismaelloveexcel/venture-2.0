# Auditbound — Offer One-Pager

## Positioning

Auditbound builds and runs AI-powered outbound systems for B2B service businesses that need more qualified sales conversations without hiring a sales team.

## Best-Fit Client

- B2B service business with 2-30 employees
- Average deal size of $2,000+
- Existing proof of delivery, but inconsistent lead flow
- Founder, owner, managing director, head of growth, or sales lead can approve campaigns quickly
- Primary markets: UK, US, Canada, Australia

## Offer Ladder

| Offer | Price | Scope | Role |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Outbound Diagnostic Sprint | $990 | ICP audit, 50 verified leads, 5-10 message variations, campaign plan | Qualify and de-risk |
| Pilot Install | $2,500 | 1 channel, 1 ICP, campaign live, basic tracking | Fast proof |
| Core System | $5,500 setup + $1,350/month, 3-month minimum | Full pipeline, reporting, weekly optimization | Main revenue offer |
| Premium | $7,500 setup + $2,500/month | Multi-ICP scaling, deeper reporting, priority optimization | Upsell only |

## Hard Boundaries

- Diagnostic means no infrastructure setup, no API connections, no domains, and no live campaign.
- Pilot is the first offer where anything goes live.
- Core is the default recommendation for qualified buyers.
- Retainer is mandatory after Core launch.

## Core Payment Terms

- 50% upfront: $2,750
- 50% before launch: $2,750
- Retainer starts at launch: $1,350/month
- Minimum retainer term: 3 months

## Guarantee

If we do not generate qualified replies or initial conversations within 30 days of launch, we continue optimizing at no cost for up to 30 additional days.

Qualified means the prospect matches the agreed ICP, responds with commercial relevance, and is not a vendor, recruiter, spammer, or unrelated contact. The client must approve required assets and send decisions within 48 hours for the guarantee to apply.

## Support Policy

90 days included after launch:

- Maximum 6 requests
- Bug fixes only
- No new campaigns, new integrations, new ICPs, copy rewrites, or strategy changes

After 90 days: $197/month support or included in an active retainer.
