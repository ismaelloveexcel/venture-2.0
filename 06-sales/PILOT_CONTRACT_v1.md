**Service Provider:** [Your Name/Company] ("Provider")

---

## SCOPE

Provider will identify and deliver a batch of **15–25 accounts** within Client's specified ICP showing **active buying window signals** (funding announcements, hiring patterns, technology stack changes, or equivalent market indicators) generated within the preceding 30 days.

For each account, Provider will deliver:
- Company profile
- Buying signal (with date and source reasoning)
- Ready-to-send outreach email template
- Reasoning document (why this account, why now)

---

## TIMELINE

- **Days 1–3:** Client provides ICP definition and approval
- **Days 4–7:** Provider delivers full batch (accounts + outreach pack)
- **Days 8–30:** Client executes outreach; Provider provides weekly outcome tracking
- **Day 31:** Review meeting; decision on continuation, refinement, or conclusion

---

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

This pilot is considered **successful** if Client generates:

- **2 or more qualified conversations** (defined as: reply indicating genuine interest + willingness to discuss further), OR
- **1 or more booked discovery calls** with decision-maker or decision-adjacent role

If **neither criterion is met**, next batch (up to 20 accounts) is provided free of charge.

- 50% ($1,250) due within 7 days of batch delivery

**Payment method:** [wire transfer / credit card / ACH — specify]

---

## SCOPE EXCLUSIONS

This pilot does NOT include:

- Account outreach execution (Client is responsible for sending)
- CRM integration or API setup
- Sales training or coaching beyond script templates
- Ongoing monitoring beyond week 4
- Lead qualification or follow-up after initial reply

---

## OUTCOME TRACKING

Client agrees to:

- Provide weekly reply/engagement data (template provided)
- Report meetings booked within 48 hours of confirmation
- Make final determination by Day 31

Provider agrees to:

- Provide simple outcome tracking template
- Answer tactical questions via email within 24 hours
- Schedule one 30-min review call at Day 31

---

## CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree not to disclose the specific account names or buying signals to third parties without written consent.

---

## TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this agreement with 5 business days written notice. If Client terminates before Day 28:

- Client is responsible for 100% of the $2,500 fee
- Remaining batch accounts are not delivered

---

## LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Provider makes no guarantee of replies, meetings, or sales. Provider is liable only for delivery of account batch + outreach templates as specified. All commercial outcomes are Client's responsibility.

---

## NEXT STEPS (if successful)

If this pilot meets success criteria, Client and Provider will discuss one of the following:

1. **Continuation:** Scaled batch (30–50 accounts/month) at negotiated rate
2. **Refinement:** Adjusted ICP + repeat pilot
3. **Transition:** Training on how to implement this process in-house
