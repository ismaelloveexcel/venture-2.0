# AI Idea Generation Prompts

Copy any of these into the Copilot Chat panel (Ctrl+Shift+I) and run them directly.

---

## PROMPT 1 — Generate 10 Niche Ideas Fast

```
I want to hit $10,000/month with a productized AI service.
Generate 10 specific niche ideas I can start within 30 days.

For each idea:
- Target customer (be specific: e.g. "solo immigration lawyers in the US")
- Core pain point
- What the AI service delivers
- Realistic monthly price
- Estimated clients needed for $10k/month
- One reason this could fail

Focus on niches where people are already spending money on the problem.
```

---

## PROMPT 2 — Stress-Test a Single Idea

```
Stress-test this venture idea as a devil's advocate investor:

IDEA: [paste your idea here]

Challenge it on:
1. Is the pain real and urgent enough to pay for?
2. Who else is solving this and how?
3. What's the hardest objection a prospect will raise?
4. What could kill this in 90 days?
5. What would have to be true for this to reach $10k/month in 90 days?

Be brutal. I need real risks, not encouragement.
```

---

## PROMPT 3 — Find My Unfair Advantage

```
Based on this background:
[paste your skills, past jobs, industries you know, languages you speak, networks you have]

What are 5 venture ideas where I have an unfair advantage over a generic freelancer?
Score each on: uniqueness of my angle, speed to first client, revenue potential.
```

---

## PROMPT 4 — Niche Down an Idea

```
I have this broad idea: [paste idea]

Help me niche it down 3 levels:
- Level 1: Broad (current version)
- Level 2: Specific niche
- Level 3: Ultra-specific micro-niche

For the Level 3 version, write a one-sentence offer and estimate what I could charge.
```

---

## PROMPT 5 — Validate Without Building

```
I want to validate this idea before writing a single line of code:
IDEA: [paste idea]

Give me:
1. A 3-question survey I can send to 10 potential customers
2. A landing page headline and subheadline to test demand
3. A cold DM/email I can send TODAY to get 3 discovery calls booked
4. The single metric that proves this idea is worth pursuing
```

---

## PROMPT 6 — Turn a Problem Into a Productized Offer

```
I keep hearing this complaint from [niche]: "[paste pain point]"

Turn this pain into a productized service offer with:
- A clear deliverable (what they get)
- A defined timeline (how long it takes)
- A price (one-time + optional monthly)
- A guarantee (reduce the risk for them)
- A one-paragraph sales pitch
```
