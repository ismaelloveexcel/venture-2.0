# Design QA Checklist

Before publishing any page, ad, or visual asset, run through this checklist.
Ask Copilot: "Review this design/copy against my design QA checklist and flag any issues."

---

## COPY & MESSAGING

- [ ] Headline passes the "5-second test" — stranger knows what you do in 5 seconds
- [ ] Sub-headline clarifies WHO it's for and WHAT they get
- [ ] No jargon, no buzzwords a prospect wouldn't use themselves
- [ ] Every claim is specific (numbers, timeframes) — no vague claims like "better results"
- [ ] CTA is action-oriented and specific ("Book My Free Call" not "Submit")
- [ ] Social proof is present (testimonial, result, logo, or case study)
- [ ] Guarantee reduces risk for the buyer

## VISUAL HIERARCHY

- [ ] One primary CTA per page — not multiple competing buttons
- [ ] Most important info is above the fold (visible without scrolling)
- [ ] Font size: headings ≥ 32px, body ≥ 16px
- [ ] Sufficient contrast ratio (WCAG AA: 4.5:1 for body text)
- [ ] White space used to guide the eye, not fill it with content

## BRAND CONSISTENCY

- [ ] Brand colours used consistently (see brand-kit.md)
- [ ] One primary font and one accent font maximum
- [ ] Tone matches brand voice (see brand-kit.md)
- [ ] Logo present and correctly sized

## MOBILE

- [ ] Tested on mobile viewport (375px width minimum)
- [ ] Buttons are minimum 44px tap target
- [ ] Text doesn't overflow or clip on small screens
- [ ] Images scale correctly

## TRUST & CREDIBILITY

- [ ] Contact info visible
- [ ] Privacy policy linked (if collecting emails)
- [ ] No broken links or placeholder text (no "[Your Name]" left in)
- [ ] Page loads in under 3 seconds

---

## Copilot Design Review Prompt

```
Review this landing page copy against my design QA checklist:

[Paste your full page copy / wireframe description here]

Check each category: Copy & Messaging, Visual Hierarchy, Brand Consistency, Mobile, Trust.
Flag every item that fails or needs improvement. Prioritise the top 3 fixes.
```
